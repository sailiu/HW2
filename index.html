About Saiyin

I moved to a mid-sized city called YinChuan, where I met My first foreign friend named Regan, who helped me initialized my study aboard plan.

I thought about drop the school, but I do not know what to do after that. I was just living, in the first two years. However, I suddenly realized that life is about under certain pressure and make it better. So I decided to double major in Finance and Information System, and graduate in the good miner. I have one and a half semester left, so I will work harder, and hopeful I could find a job that I am satisfied with.
